Welcome to RoomFinder!

RoomFinder is an web app to help individuals quickly find classrooms, restrooms, elevators and stairways within University of Michigan buildings. It is for students, staff, and anyone who needs to navigate GG Brown, who are dissatisfied with attempting to find rooms with occasionally posted physical maps or signs. Unlike Google Maps, RoomFinder focuses on navigating inside of buildings, not just on getting you to the building itself.

Try searching for an existing room within GG Brown!
